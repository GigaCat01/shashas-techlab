BeatFall Privacy Policy

← Back to Home

Last Updated: May 17, 2026

Shasha's Techlab ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in relation to our mobile application, BeatFall.

1. Information We Collect

BeatFall is designed as a localized, offline-first experience. We do not collect, store, or share any personal information, such as your name, email address, or contact details.

  • Local Audio Processing: All artificial intelligence beat transcription and audio processing is performed strictly locally on your device. Your music files are never uploaded to our servers or third-party servers.
  • Direct Data: We do not monitor, record, or track the audio files you choose to play.

2. Third-Party Services

Currently, BeatFall operates completely independently and does not integrate third-party advertisement networks or remote analytics SDKs that track your usage.

3. Permissions

The app requires specific permissions to function correctly:

  • Storage / Media Access (READ_MEDIA_AUDIO): Required to access your local music library so that you can select songs to play within the game.

4. Data Retention

As we do not collect personal data, we do not retain any user information on our servers. Game high scores and localized analysis cache are saved entirely on your device's internal storage.

5. Children's Privacy

Our services do not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at the email provided in the Google Play Store listing.
