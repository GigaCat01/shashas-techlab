Shasha's Techlab ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in relation to our mobile applications, specifically KinBo Comic Converter.

1. Information We Collect

KinBo Comic Converter is designed to respect user privacy. We do not collect, store, or share any personal information, such as your name, email address, or contact details.

  • Local Processing: All comic conversion is performed locally on your device. Your files are never uploaded to our servers.
  • Direct Data: We do not have access to the files you convert or the output generated.

2. Third-Party Services

Our app incorporates third-party services that may collect information used to identify you or your device:

  • Google AdMob: Used to display advertisements. AdMob may collect device identifiers (like Android Advertising ID) to provide personalized ads and analytics.
  • Google Play Billing: Used for in-app purchases (e.g., removing ads). All payment transactions are handled securely by Google Play; we never see or store your financial information.

3. Permissions

The app requires specific permissions to function:

  • Storage Access: To read your comic files and save the converted output.
  • Internet: To load advertisements and verify in-app purchases.
  • Notifications: To keep you informed of conversion progress when the app is in the background.

4. Data Retention

As we do not collect personal data, we do not retain any user information on our servers. Any temporary files created during conversion are deleted immediately upon completion or cancellation.

5. Children's Privacy

Our services do not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at the email provided in the Google Play Store listing.
